About the Website

Nanotechnology, Programming, and Creative Pursuits

Welcome

Welcome to my website! I'm Dr. Siavash Bakhtiarnia, a researcher with a passion for nanotechnology and a curious mind that loves exploring various subjects. This website serves as a comprehensive resource showcasing my work in both scientific research and creative endeavors.

You can view my detailed professional background in my Resume.

Dive into my research articles, explore coding projects, or get inspired by my creative writing!

Content

This section explores my endeavors in two primary areas: scientific research and creative pursuits.

  • Research

    My research focuses on Photocatalytic Water Treatment and Solar Energy. I share my scientific articles published in peer-reviewed journals, along with general posts to make the wonders and applications of nanotechnology accessible to a wider audience.

  • Code

    The code section offers tutorials, simple coding projects, and code snippets. It also features an exciting "code playground" where viewers can directly run and interact with the code. Explore coding with "Introduction to Programming" to learn about your first code ("Hello, World!"), loops, if statements, and eye-catching visualizations. Coding is an exciting area, and I love connecting with other enthusiasts to contribute and learn together.

  • Explorations

    The exploration section delves into my adventures, including exciting trips, museum visits, creative writing (poetry, short stories), photography, and short films. It's a space for anything I'm passionate about and want to share.

I'm constantly expanding this website with high-quality posts in the field of nanotechnology, exciting creative content, and inspiring programming tutorials. Stay tuned!

Audience

This website caters to a diverse audience, including students, researchers, and anyone curious about nanotechnology, programming, and creative exploration.

Explore More

In addition to the content on this website, you can also explore my coding blog, "My Code Universe," which offers informative and entertaining content featuring Python code and Jupyter Notebooks.
