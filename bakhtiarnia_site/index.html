About me

My journey in Nanotechnology began with a Master's degree from Sharif University of Technology. This passion culminated in my Ph.D. research at Tehran University, where I focused on advancements in visible-light-driven photocatalysis. My expertise lies in leveraging nanomaterials and solar energy to address water treatment challenges. I specialize in developing thin-film photocatalysts, particularly using Bismuth Vanadate (BiVO4) deposited through physical vapor deposition, a method not widely explored for this application. This field holds immense potential for real-world impact, and I'm passionate about continuing to explore innovative techniques to develope sustainable water treatment solutions - that's what truly motivates me.

Welcome to the World of Nanotechnology

Step into a world where scientists are turning science fiction into reality. My research focuses on developing nanomaterials for purifying water using sunlight (photocatalytic water treatment) and creating thin films with precise properties (physical vapor deposition). These advancements hold immense potential for creating a more sustainable future.

Whether you're a student, researcher, or simply curious about the power of the miniscule, this platform offers accessible content that explores the transformative power of nanotechnology. Dive deeper into my publications and posts to learn how nanomaterials can revolutionize clean energy, environmental remediation, and more.

Let's demystify the complexities of nanotechnology together and explore the quantum realm that grants these materials their remarkable properties. Join the conversation, enrich your understanding, and help us shape a brighter future.
